## Important Context

**Why not jump to mainnet now?**
- Sepolia is the right proving ground (free failures)
- Need to find all edge cases before real money
- SLA infrastructure must be rock-solid first
- 1,000+ requests will give us 6-12 month competitive advantage

**Why bifurcated pricing (Base vs L1)?**
- Base Mainnet: High volume ($0.05-0.35), AI agents
- Ethereum L1: Premium ($0.50-$5.00), institutions
- Different markets, different willingness to pay

**Why debate matters more than verdict?**
- Verdict is "noise" (easy to fake)
- Debate is "signal" (hard to fake, full reasoning)
- Institutional clients need to audit reasoning
- Will charge 3x more for debate transcript
